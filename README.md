# Savya Sanchi Sharma

BTech undergraduate specializing in Data Science and Artificial Intelligence, focused on building intelligent systems with a strong foundation in systems programming and performance-oriented design.

## Profile

I work at the intersection of machine learning and low-level computing. My interests span AI/ML, backend systems, generative AI and low level systems .i.e. edge devices  . I am particularly driven by understanding how modern systems work under the hood and optimizing them for efficiency and scalability.

## Current Direction

- Deepening expertise in Rust and backend engineering
- Experimenting with Generative AI systems
- Exploring CUDA and performance-aware computing
- Studying compiler development to understand programming language internals
- Building projects that integrate Web Development, AI, and Blockchain
